Sherrod Brown

Ohio State Senator



Key Issues:

  • Economy and Jobs: Senator Brown fights for economic policies that assist middle class workers. He agrees with the idea to rebuild “American Manufacturing” and the creation of small businesses for new industry.

  • Manufacturing: Senator Brown has worked and is still working on making partnerships across Ohio. He has also worked on protecting Ohio in unfair trade and continues in the protection of Ohio’s Manufacturing businesses.

Committees:

Background:

Born on November 9, 1952, in Mansfield, Ohio, Senator Sherrod Brown atteneded Yale University before taking office in 2007.
