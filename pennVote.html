Pennsylvania
In Pennsylvania a person must be registered to vote at least 1 month before the actual election date. A person has to register at the Pennsylvania Department of State or the Pennsylvania Department of Transportation. Pennsylvania does NOT have an online registration.To vote in the primary election which determines the candidate that will represent each party, a person must be a registered member of that party.
