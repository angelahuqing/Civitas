Diane Feinstein

California State Senator



Key Issues:

  • Economy: Senator Feinstein believes that Congress has a responsibility to invest in creating jobs through “job training programs, investing in infrastructure and making higher education more affordable.” She also believes in passing legislation on tax reform and reducing congressional spending to balance the federal budget.

  • Drugs: Senator Feinstein is the chairman of the Senate Caucus on International Narcotics Control. She is interested in reducing “trafficking and drug-related violence in Mexico and Afghanistan,” and the effect that illegal drugs have in communities in the United states.

  • Environment: Senator Feinstein strongly believes in the preservation of natural resources and protecting our environment. She has been a constant champion for legislation that would lower humans carbon footprint on the environment. “one of her top priorities each year is robust funding for research and development of renewable energy technologies and energy efficiency improvements.”
