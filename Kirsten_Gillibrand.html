Kirsten Gillibrand

New York State Senator



Key Issues:

  • Economy: Senator Gillibrand believes in better pay for people, creating products that would be made by american workers, and creating opportunities for more small local New york businesses.

  • Health care: Senator Gillibrand believes in expanding and protecting the access that people have to healthcare. She also believes in the key aspects of the Affordable Care Act including legislation that bans insurance companies from discriminating against people based on pre existing conditions, health status, and gender.
