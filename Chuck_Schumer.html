Chuck Schumer

New York State Senator



Key Issues:

  • Immigration Modernization Act: Schumer sponsored this act to help immigrants and refugees come to the United States without trouble and get social security.

  • College Tuition: Schumer created a $4,000 tax credit to middle class families to offset college tuition cost.
